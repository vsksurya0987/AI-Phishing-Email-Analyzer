================================================================================
COMMON PHISHING INDICATORS
================================================================================

• Creates urgency
• Threatens account suspension
• Requests passwords
• Requests OTPs
• Requests banking information
• Uses unknown domains
• Uses fake login pages
• Requests immediate payment
• Uses fear tactics
• Creates pressure
• Uses shortened URLs
• Requests confidential information

================================================================================
BEST PRACTICES
================================================================================

• Never click unknown links.
• Verify the sender's email.
• Check the domain carefully.
• Enable Multi-Factor Authentication.
• Never share OTPs.
• Report suspicious emails.
• Contact the organization through official channels.
